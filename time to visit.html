-------------------------------Festivals in Pyin Oo Lwin------------------------------


Tazaungtine (Fire Balloons) Festival



In recent years, however, Pyin Oo Lwin has also gained attention for its annual fire balloon festival, which will be held for the 10th time later this year.

The festival, which is held in the five days leading up to the full moon of Tazaungdaing - normally October or November - draws thousands to the town at the end of the rainy season, when the chill of winter is beginning to descend.

While the crowds are not as large as those at the Taunggyi balloon festival, held in the Shan State capital each year during Tazaungdaing, they continue to grow each year.

The festival is held on a field on the edge of town near Maha Ant Htoo Kan Thar Pagoda. As a result of the number of spectators, authorities have expanded the field in recent years to cover 40 acres. The 13th Pyin Oo Lwin Tazaungdaing Balloon Festival in Mandalay Region will be held from October 31 to November 4 at the Maha Ant Htoo Kan Thar Pagoda ground.

This year, the Tazaungdine festival has attracted the interest of professional photographers from across the country and around the world. Professional photographers will compete in both a day and night photo contest. The competitions will award prizes in both categories.

The festival will start at 8am on 11 November with a small aircraft demonstration. Hot-air balloons will be released the same day at 12pm and the night hot-air balloons will be released at 6pm.

Details

Location - Near Maha Ant Htoo Kan Thar Pagoda
Time - Mostly in November or October
Duration - 5 Days
Other Events - Theatrical Entertainments, Fair
Compititions - Photography, Beautiful Fireballoons

Flowers Festival


Pyin Oo Lwin Flower Festival will be lengthened from its usual two weeks to span the entire month of December.

This will be the festival's 12th year running in the scenic town in Mandalay Region. The festival will be held at the 437-acre National Kandawgyi Botanical Garden, which has been open since 1915.

Mission is to increase in the number of visitors to the Flower Festival at the garden every year. Each year, visitors to the festival number in the hundreds of thousands.

The festival will display more than 130,000 flower species, including local and foreign strains. There will also be floral sculptures on display, and vendors will sell local handicrafts. Last year, the festival also included rock and hip hop concerts on certain nights.

Admission to the festival costs K1,000 per adult and K500 per child for Myanmar nationals and US$5 for foreigners.

Details

Location - National Kandawgyi Garden
Time - In December, near X'mas
Duration - 2 Weeks
Other Events - Stage Show, Fund-fair
Compititions - Photography
