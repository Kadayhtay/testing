National Landmarks Garden

Just beside the Kandawgyi National Botanical Gardens is another must-see attraction, the National Landmarks Garden.It consists of scale models of well-known landmarks from each state in Myanmar, including its most famous pagodas , spread over 50acres of parkland. There is a mini theme park and a huge garden. It is established in 2006 with the aims to be able to observe ancient cultural edifices, pagodas and stupas , places, caves , hills, and mountains can be viewed round collectively in the garden. This is an interesting place that has miniatures of all the landmarks in Myanmar, reproduced in miniatures. It is nice to see everything in one place and the gardens are very nicely maintained.Visitors can relax and obtain general knowledge, while enjoying the typical style of living of the national races living in the states and divisions during their visit. Local visitors are charged Ks 1000 for an adult and Ks 500 for a child as entry fees, wheareas it is US $10 for an adult and $5 for a child for foreign visitors. This place is very beautiful and peaceful.You can breathe fresh air there. You can live with your family happily.So, you should visit this place.



Location

In front Kandawgyi Garden, Nabdar Road,
Pyin Oo Lwin, Mandalay Region, Myanmar.

 Details

   Time : 8:00 am - 7:00 pm

  Fees : MMK 1000
