Mr.Yang Xing Chao

Founder of Chan Tak Temple

-------------------- About/History --------------------

With a strong Yunnanese Chinese population due to an influx of immigration, Pyin Oo Lwin has a large Buddhist Temple where many local people worship. This colorful structure, built by the Yunnanese people, provides a nice location for a walk just south of the town and shows another outside influence in the small yet diverse town.

Buddhist Temple (also called Chinese Temple or Chinese Vegetarian Temple) was built over 30 years ago.The founder of it is Mr.Yang Xing Chao, who is the head of regional chinese monastic association.He died last 5 years ago.At that time,he had over 80 pupils but now just 50 pupils remain.There is vegetarian restaurant inside temple area.
