Htoo Orange Farm


Location

No. 691, Pin Lain Village,
near Pwe Gauk Waterfall, Pyin Oo Lwin.

Details

Time: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Fees: 2000Ks
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/HtooAgri/

About

First time visiting an orange farm distributing in local, opened for public not long ago. It costs 2000 ks entrance fee, including a glass of pure refreshing orange juice.It's a very nice place to take photos, can harvest orange by our own and buy them in kilos but there aren't much things to do there. Suggest to go there in the evening. The location is quite far from the town, near the Hempshire waterfalls(Pwel kouk).One of the largest orange plantations done methodically. You can have a ride around the plantation. Also you are allowed to pluck oranges. After the oranges are weighted and you pay an affordable price. Also for the cost of the ticket you get free orange juices too. You also find dragon fruit plants as well.


Direction


Firstly, you go to the Pwe Gauk Waterfall. If you drive from the Pwe Gauk Waterfall , acrossing the Pwe Gauk Village & railway road,you will see a monastery. You must drive the rightside road of the monestery and after driving about over 10 minutes from there, you will reach Pin Lain Village and then see Htoo Orange Farm.

Recommendation

We recommend for the following reasons :
  • Handpicking the oranges by yourself
  • Taking the beautiful photos
  • The sweet oranges
  • Getting knowledge of orange factory
