Description

The Sky bar is the best place to watch the sun set in Pyin Oo Lwin. Located on the 6th floor of the luxury hotel Shwe Nann Htike, this restaurant and bar has something for everyone: cold beer, amazing views, a long cocktail list, and delicious food inspired by Chinese, Burmese, and Thai traditional cooking.
