Nearby Towns

Mandalay


 Mandalay is about 69km away from Pyin Oo Lwin. It is the second-largest city and the last royal capital of Myanmar (Burma).Mandalay is Burma's cultural and religious center of Buddhism, having numerous monasteries and more than 700 pagodas. Mandalay is the economic centre of Upper Burma and considered the centre of Burmese culture......for more detail,click here

Mogok


 Mogok is a city in the Pyin Oo Lwin District of the Mandalay Region of Myanmar, located 200 km north of Mandalay and 148 km north-east of Shwebo. Mogok was in Shan State and after British, the town was put in Mandalay Region along with Pyin Oo Lwin. Mogok is also referred to as the "Valley of Rubies", a name made famous by the novel Mogok, the Valley of Rubies by Joseph Kessel and Stella Rodway......for more detail,click here

Northern Shan State

 Northern Shan State, For an easy escape from the heat and hussle of Mandalay, do what the colonial Brits did and pop up to Pyin Oo Lwin.
