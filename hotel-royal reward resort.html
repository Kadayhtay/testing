Welcome To Royal Reward Resort


Resort Description

Ideal for fun and relaxation, Royal Reward Resort is located in the Pyin Oo Lwin City Center area of Pyin Oo Lwin. The excitement of the city center is only 1.0 Miles away. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

Royal Reward Resort also offers many facilities to enrich your stay in Pyin Oo Lwin. This hotel offers numerous on-site facilities to satisfy even the most discerning guest.

The hotel features 23 beautifully appointed guest rooms, many of which include television LCD/plasma screen, additional bathroom, additional toilet, carpeting, complimentary instant coffee. The hotel offers an excellent variety of recreational facilities, including hot tub, fitness center, golf course (within 3 km), massage, garden. With an ideal location and facilities to match, Royal Reward Resort hits the spot in many ways.
