Highway Road


 Review


The Pyin Oo Lwin-Lashio part of Highway Road became popular since 2016 because of a smooth and straight part of road and conifer forests which are situated each side of road.Many travellers especailly local guests come there,take photos and then it becomes the one of popular places in Pyin Oo Lwin.The Mdy-Pol part of road is almost finished and will become popular too.

Direction


The strat point of this beautiful road is situated about 16 miles far from mandalay.And at 37 milestone of the Mandalay-Lashio Road from Mandalay, there is a junction. Turn right at there and go ahead. After driving about 7 miles, there is a wonderful road with conifer forests on both sides and it looks like a Western Country's Road.
