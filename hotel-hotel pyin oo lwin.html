Welcome To Hotel Pyin Oo Lwin


Resort Description

Situated in Pyin Oo Lwin City Center, Hotel Pyin Oo Lwin is the perfect place to experience Pyin Oo Lwin and its surroundings. From here, guests can enjoy easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

Hotel Pyin Oo Lwin also offers many facilities to enrich your stay in Pyin Oo Lwin. The hotel offers access to a vast array of services, including free Wi-Fi in all rooms, Wi-Fi in public areas, car park, room service, airport transfer.

Hotel Pyin Oo Lwin is home to 36 bedrooms. All are tastefully furnished and many even provide such comforts as television LCD/plasma screen, internet access – wireless (complimentary), air conditioning, desk, mini bar. The hotel offers fantastic facilities, including fitness center, indoor pool, garden, to help you unwind after an action-packed day in the city. Hotel Pyin Oo Lwin is your one-stop destination for quality hotel accommodations in Pyin Oo Lwin.
