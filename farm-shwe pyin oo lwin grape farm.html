Shwe Pyin Oo Lwin Grape Farm

Location

Shwe Thin Village, Pyin Oo Lwin,
Mandalay Region, Myanmar.

Details

Time: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Fees: No fee charged
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/shwepyinoolwin888/



Pyin Oo Lwin,Mandalay Division,Shwe Pyin Oo Lwin vineyard is owned by Shan State native Thein Win and situated on 5 acres of land in Shwe Thin village and is opened last month to visitors. A 45-minutes drive from downtown Pyin Oo Lwin over a bumpy road delivers guests from the town to rows of grape-bearing vines. U Thein Win started it in 2013 and have tried various seeds and methods to achieve different tastes. The results have been positive so far.




He cultivates four grape varietals from Japan, Taiwan, China and the United States, respectively. After three years of preparation, this was the first year the grapes were ready to pick.Although they had a successful crop last year, they did not invite people to handpick until this year when there were more grapes to sell.He now sells at least 5000 viss (one viss is approximately 3.6 pounds) of grapes each day, at 10,000 kyats per viss for handpicked grapes.




If his workers help pick grapes for visitors , he only charge 8000 kyats per viss; but for handpicked grapes, he charged an extra 2000 kyat because he factor in some loss. On the weekends, groups line up along the grapes vines with bamboo baskets and picks as much as they like, selecting grapes of various colors and sizes. He expects to expand his vineyard even more in the coming months and years. Picking grapes was a relaxing escape from the stress of urban living. It is a good feeling to pick the grapes before eating them, and also a great spot to take pictures.

Direction


If you come from Kandawgyi, acrosss the highway road and go ahead the street to the Sin Gaung Lay Village and turn right & go ahead. After driving about 15 minutes (3.5miles) , you will see the Shwe Pyin Oo Lwin Grape Farm at left side. If you come from Highway road, you must drive 10 minutes(6miles) & turn right if you see the access road of Sin Gaung Lay Village & go like as about.

Recommendation

We recommend for the following reasons :
  • Handpicking the grape by yourself
  • Taking the beautiful photos
  • The sweet grapes
  • Feel the country style
