Welcome To Royal Parkview Hotel


Resort Description

Nestled in the heart of Pyin Oo Lwin City Center, Royal Parkview Hotel is an ideal spot from which to discover Pyin Oo Lwin. From here, guests can enjoy easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

At Royal Parkview Hotel, every effort is made to make guests feel comfortable. To do so, the hotel provides the best in services and amenities. A selection of top-class facilities such as 24-hour room service, free Wi-Fi in all rooms, 24-hour security, daily housekeeping, fireplace can be enjoyed at the hotel.

Royal Parkview Hotel is home to 30 bedrooms. The hotel offers fantastic facilities, including golf course (within 3 km), massage, horse riding, garden, to help you unwind after an action-packed day in the city. Whatever your reason for visiting Pyin Oo Lwin, the Royal Parkview Hotel is the perfect venue for an exhilarating and exciting break away.
