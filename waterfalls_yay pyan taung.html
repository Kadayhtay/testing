Yay Pyan Taung Waterfall

About
The waterfall of Yay Pyan Taung is a real treasure. Its best described as a collection of countless creeks and cascades large and small streaming out of the ravine. The impressive views of waterfall are worth to enjoy or take a memorable picture. The volume of water is particularly immense in the raining season. On the way back to home, you can rest at Shwe Pyin Oo Lwin vineyard to pick a full of basket with fresh grapes and can also drink cold drinks.

Review

Great for a couple of hours of calm to escape the hustle and bustle. The orchids were out of season and the butterfly museum was just that, glass cases of pinned examples. The fruit tree area and the aviary were very good as were the petrified wood examples.
