Royal Rose Garden

Location

Inn Htate Oo Village, Pyin Oo Lwin,
Mandalay Region, Myanmar.

Details

Time: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Fees: No fee charged

About

According to The Ultimate Rose Book, the rose was introduced in 1983, when Britain celebrated the birth of Prince William, second in line to the British throne. The Royal William rose has been planted in Windsor Great Park's Savill Garden in celebration of the weeding of Prince William and Catherine Middleton. A rose is a woody perrnnial flowering plant of the genus Rosa, in the family Rosaceae, or the flower it bears. There are over three hundred species and thousands of cultvars. Different species hybridize easily,and this has been used in the development of the wide range of garden roses. Roses are very beautiful and colourful.This place is very beautiful and peaceful.You can breathe fresh air there. You can live with your family happily.So, you should visit this place. The rose garden is a wonderful place to stroll through the roses and then relax in one of the cozy covered seating areas and enjoy the breez and view. Even though we were there during the hot season in mid-afternoon, it wasn't too hot to enjoy the environment. It is very enjoyable place.


Direction


If you come from Kandawgyi, acrosss the highway road and go ahead the street to the Inn Htate Oo Village and turn right & go ahead. After driving about 20 minutes (4miles) , you will see the Royal Rose Garden at right side. If you come from Highway road, you must drive 10 minutes(6miles) & turn left if you see the access road of Inn Htate Oo Village & go like as about.
