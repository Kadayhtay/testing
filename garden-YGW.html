YGW Garden

YGW Garden,Maymyo , one of the nice places , should visit when we visit Pyin Oo Lwin and the idea of owner is amazing.Visitors can rest and walk on cross country.And then for animal lovers, it has rabbit, duck , turkey, monkey, bear, hornbill , etc there.Moveover, horses and dogs are in garden.If you want to photograph, it has the beautiful places.The most beautiful places are American flag and Myanmar flag on the lookout. And barbarian is attractive. Antiques , animals skin, fossils are shown in YGW garden. And the meals that can get in garden are also famous. The most famous meals are raw potato salad and fried rice.Visitors are not fee charged. And YGW garden is very beautiful and has interested things. It has the most beautiful place to take photos.So, if you visit Pyin Oo Lwin, you should go to the YGW garden.




Location

Nyaung Pin Thar Village,
Pyin Oo Lwin, Mandalay Region, Myanmar.
