Welcome To Aureum Palace Hotel

Resort Description

Nestled in the heart of Pyin Oo Lwin City Center, Aureum Palace Hotel & Resort is an ideal spot from which to discover Pyin Oo Lwin. From here, guests can enjoy easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

Aureum Palace Hotel & Resort also offers many facilities to enrich your stay in Pyin Oo Lwin. Guests of the hotel can enjoy on-site features like 24-hour room service, 24-hour security, daily housekeeping, taxi service, ticket service.

Aureum Palace Hotel & Resort is home to 40 bedrooms. All are tastefully furnished and many even provide such comforts as television LCD/plasma screen, clothes rack, complimentary instant coffee, complimentary tea, mirror. The hotel offers fantastic facilities, including outdoor pool, to help you unwind after an action-packed day in the city. Whatever your reason for visiting Pyin Oo Lwin, the Aureum Palace Hotel & Resort is the perfect venue for an exhilarating and exciting break away.
