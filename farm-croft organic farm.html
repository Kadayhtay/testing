The Croft Organic Farm

Location

Near Kandawgyi Garden, Pyin Oo Lwin,
Mandalay Region, Myanmar.

Details

Time: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Fees: 2500Ks
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/thecroftorganicfarm/



The successful operation of a small farm that utilizes best practices to optimize the available resources and sharing our knowledge with others. To strength and expand organic practices and which reduce the use of toxic, synthetic chemicals and have clear benefits to human health and the environment. The Croft Organic Farm is founded with a motivation to raise the habit of consuming organic foods and maintain the ecological stability.




As fruits are seasonal, you will feel the exciting fruit picking experience every time you visit the croft organic farm. When you stroll around the croft organic farm, you can see seasonal crop plantations in every corner of the farm.As the seasonal crop rotation, organic tomotoes are in season and ready to be harvested. Tomatoes can be consumed in diverse ways, including raw, as an ingredient in many dishes, salads and sauces.




The most fascinating and picturesque places in the Croft Organic farm are green strawberry plantation, Red Farm House, the colourful umbrella walkway, dragon fruits trellis and the row of tomatoes plants. The Croft Organic Farm is just like the real natural sanctuary in which ecological balance is maintianed to have benefits to the environment and human Health.




As you walk into the Croft Organic Farm, you will see colourful and fascinating umbrella walkway. You can escape the heat of the burning sun'rays and take a good rest while sipping your coffee and snack.




Hello foodies, Have you ever tried "The Croft Organic Farm's Pizza"? If not, you can order now at the croft organic farm while there is still, Buy 1 Get 1 Promotion. From 12 pizza toppings and different flavors, you can choose your favourite one.Just one piece can satisfy your pizza craving. So come and enjoy "The Croft Organic Farm'Pizza".Guess what will be the new Menu in Farm. Green Tea Leaf Salad with 100% organic Tomatoes. La Phet Thote is very traditional and one of the most favourite of our people. I am sure you will love it. And the Croft Organic Farm's cupcake recipe is got in this place.This cupcake taste is very good.




Mini Zoo is the most interesting spot for kids and visitors. It is sure that kids are fascinated by the cute little bunnies. Little bunnies with red eyes are so tame and adorable. They have plan to build bird cage to be more attractive and pleasanter in the farm later on.


Direction

If you walk about 2 minutes from gate of Maymyo Botanical Garden (National Kandawgyi Park) and National Landmark,Pyin Oo Lwin, you wil see 'The Croft Organic Farm'.
