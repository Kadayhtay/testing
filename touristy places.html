Pagodas

      Pyin Oo Lwin has a good number of pagodas.Most are in good condition and are well looked after.You can get well-worth seeing from them because almost pagodas are situated on the hill.Few,if any,have hostrical significance or can rival the spectular pagodas in Myanmar.

  1. Maha Ant Htoo Kan Thar
  2. Kyauk Taung Pagoda
  3. Pate Chin Myaung Cave
  4. Maha Hsu Taung Pyae Nhee Buddha Image
  5.  

Waterfalls

      Waterfalls in Pyin Oo Lwin are very pleasant picnic spots and famous for their natural beauty and breathtaking secenry.Almost they are a little far from downtown.You can swim and play in the shallow waters.Most of them have a market besides them where you can buy local products.

  1. Pwe Gauk Waterfall
  2. Dattaw Gyaink Waterfall
  3. Dee Dote Waterfall
  4. Yay Pyan Taung Waterfall
  5. December Waterfall

Garden

      Gardens in Pyin Oo Lwin are unique in Myanmar.They have wide areas and shows a large variety of floras,flowers and trees.Every garden has at least one museum and you can study about botanical cases,get knowledges of Myanmar's national races and also see many creative things.

  1. National Kan Daw Gyi Garden
  2. National Landmark Garden
  3. YGW Garden
  4. December Garden

Farm

      Many local products of Pyin Oo Lwin are grown on natives' own farm.When you reach its farms,you can feel like reaching on western country side.By visiting farms of Pyin Oo Lwin,you can buy their regional goods cheaper than the local gift shops.

  1. Htoo Orange Farm
  2. Shwe Pyin Oo Lwin Grape Farm
  3. Royal Rose Farm
  4. The Croft Organic Farm

Colonial Buildings

      There are many British colonial houses and Christian churchs in Pyin Oo Lwin. Most of the colonial houses are situated at the south end of the circular road.Now,most of them are turned into schools,government offices and hotels.You can get a feel of british influnce from them.

  1. Govenor's House
  2. Ancient Villas(Myaing)
  3. Colonial Churches
  4.  

Other Famous Places

      Another famous places for guests are also situated at the Hillside stairs town(Pyin Oo Lwin). You can get many beautiful and attraction photos with them.You can also taste many delicious foods at them.Have a great time at the city of flowers!

  1. Chinese Temple
  2. Night Market
  3. Cozy World
  4. Pine Forest Highway
